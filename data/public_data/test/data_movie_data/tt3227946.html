Storyline

Charles an architect after enduring an accident in which he loses his family moves home. His new neighborhood is quaint and strange with lots of interesting characters. He attends a dinner party organized by Sally his neighbor where he meets them all including a Gothic spiritual healer called Agatha. He is having nightmares about his family's accident. He then has to undergo seven sin trials which threaten lives of his family members in which many of his family members die over and over again with different events leading up to each death based on choices he makes. Which make him question whether there really is such a thing as accidental death? Written by Jill Thrussell
