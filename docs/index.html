Goals and Features

People nowadays love to watch movie.

However, ordinary people may not have enough information about the casts or directors’ style to make an informed choice.

We aim to solve this problem by developing an android application where users can view most movie ratings from various movie websites. By providing their own ratings (user input), we will analyse the similarity between users’ input and existing ratings to create an individualised rating reference.
